Welcome to Travel Eelam

Coming from a history of struggle, resilience and perserverance, the Tamil people from Sri Lanka have faced years of discrimination and hardship, especially during the Sri Lankan Civil War. But even through all of this, our identity was never lost - it was held onto, protected and passed down through generations. Today, Tamil culture is still alive and thriving, with a rich heritage.

Discover the beauty of Tamil heritage, popular landmarks, food, and culture that make Tamil Eelam, an unforgettable land to visit.

Featured Top Landmarks

Explore our handpicked selection of the top landmarks in Tamil Eelam - click on each image to discover more.

Nilaveli Beach

Discover the serene beauty of Nilaveli Beach, a pristine coastal paradise in Sri Lanka's Jaffna peninsula, known for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine life.

Kumana National Park

Discover the rich biodiversity of Kumana National Park, a wildlife sanctuary in Sri Lanka's southeast coast, known for its diverse bird species and vibrant ecosystems.

Mannar Fort

Discover the historical significance of Mannar Fort, a colonial-era fortress in Sri Lanka's Mannar district, known for its strategic location and rich history.

Mullivaikkal Memorial

Discover the significance of the Mullivaikkal Memorial, a poignant site in Sri Lanka's Jaffna peninsula, dedicated to the memory of those who lost their lives during the final stages of the Sri Lankan Civil War.

Kanniya Hot Springs

Discover the therapeutic benefits of Kanniya Hot Springs, a natural wonder in Sri Lanka's eastern coast, known for its mineral-rich waters and relaxing atmosphere.

Nallur, Jaffna

Discover the rich history and culture of Nallur, a vibrant town in Jaffna known for its ancient temples and bustling markets.
